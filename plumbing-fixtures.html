Commercial Plumbing Fixtures — Sourcing for Bathrooms & Kitchens

Enhancing Spaces with Precision Plumbing Solutions

Phipps Global has worked with hotels, resorts, and building developers around the world to source kitchen and bathroom commercial plumbing fixtures. From modern faucets and showerheads to sleek sinks and accessories, our fixtures are made with precision and durability in mind.

Craftsmanship and Style in Every Fixture

Phipps Global offers expertly crafted plumbing fixtures designed to enhance both functionality and aesthetics. Available in various finishes and styles, our products cater to diverse design preferences while ensuring exceptional performance and reliability. Whether for a contemporary or classic setting, our plumbing fixtures deliver a perfect balance of innovation and timeless appeal, transforming spaces with quality and style.

Unparalleled Products and Procurement

As one of the top commercial building developers with a global reach, we procure materials from over 18 countries worldwide, tailoring them to meet your specific project requirements. Our close partnerships with factories grant us access to priority production lines, ensuring timely delivery and unwavering support.

Unmatched Pricing and Results

Our client base includes builders, architects, designers, hoteliers, and general contractors in the multifamily, affordable housing, commercial, residential, and hospitality sectors. With our deep understanding of production processes, sourcing locations, and pricing, we empower our clients to achieve their project goals within their budgetary constraints.
