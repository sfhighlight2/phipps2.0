Our Philosophy

The Phipps Philosophy

Phipps Global stands out as a leading name among commercial building developers with a global footprint, offering comprehensive factory-direct development services for a diverse range of projects. Based in Florida, we extend our expertise across the USA and internationally, ensuring that every hotel, residential complex, and commercial facility meets the highest standards of quality and design.

Our approach is to create value through innovation and efficiency, sourcing materials from over 18 countries to deliver projects that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also economically advantageous. Our clients trust us to bring their vision to life with precision and care.

With a deep understanding of the intricacies of commercial development, Phipps Global is dedicated to achieving excellence. We navigate the complexities of production processes, sourcing, and pricing to empower our clients, helping them to fulfill their project ambitions within their financial framework.
