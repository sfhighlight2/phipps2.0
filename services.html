Exquisite Tile & Stone

Top Quality Flooring

Solid wood, engineered wood, LVT, and vinyl flooring options for every residential or commercial application. Factory-direct manufacturing ensures competitive pricing without sacrificing quality.

Exceptional Countertops

Granite, marble, quartz, solid surface, porcelain slab — Phipps Global provides countertops manufactured to exact specifications for kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial applications.

Multifunctional and outdoor furniture designed and manufactured to your exact specifications. From lobby seating to guestroom case goods, every piece is built to last.

Stylish Bathroom Furnishings

Finishing Touches

FF&E, accessories, and lighting — the details that transform a space from functional to extraordinary. We source and manufacture everything from decorative lighting to custom hardware.

08

Bespoke Millwork

Custom millwork manufactured to your exact specifications. From hotel headboards to residential cabinetry, our factory partners deliver precision craftsmanship.
